Watches vs. Warnings
Two different levels of urgency
Watch

Conditions Favorable

Atmosphere is primed. Prepare now — know your shelter, charge your phone, stay tuned.

Warning

Imminent Threat

Severe weather is occurring or about to. Act immediately — seek shelter now.

tornado
Enhanced Fujita Scale
Tornado intensity by damage
EF0
65–85 mph

Minor Damage

Broken branches, chimneys. Little structural damage.

EF1
86–110 mph

Moderate Damage

Roofs peeled off. Mobile homes overturned.

EF2
111–135 mph

Considerable Damage

Homes badly damaged. Large trees snapped.

EF3
136–165 mph

Severe Damage

Homes destroyed. Cars lifted and thrown.

EF4
166–200 mph

Devastating Damage

Well-built homes leveled. Cars thrown far.

EF5
>200 mph

Incredible Damage

Structures swept away. Catastrophic destruction.
